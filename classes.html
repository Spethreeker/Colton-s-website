Classes We Offer:

Etiquette

Our class on etiquette will teach your poodle the proper way to act in a crowd and teach them to act proper around other dogs instead of their natural instinct.

Observing

Our class on observing will teach your poodle to behave no matter what situations are taking place around them. We will teach them to heel when told and your poodle will not move until told by the trainer.

Bark Control

Our bark control class will teach your dog to control barking unless there is an intruder in the house or if they sense danger and intend to warn you.

Attack Methods

Our attack method class will teach your poodle the proper way to use force to protect their owner. We will use different poodle attack methods and styles to train your poodle. Poodle-itsu is our main fighting style to train your dog.
